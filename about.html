Who Are We?

We are Christ-centered, interdenominational fellowship that empowers young people to navigate spiritual,professional, and social challenges through Biblical principles and the transformative power of faith.

Empowering the next generation to walk in faith, guided by His Word, and equipped for life's journey with wisdom and purpose.

Kato C. Lafont, Founder

Feed My Lambs Youth Ministry (FLY-M) is an interdenominational fellowship dedicated to guiding young people in their spiritual, professional, and social journeys. Rooted in Biblical principles, FLY-M aims to help youth build a personal relationship with Jesus while empowering them to navigate life’s challenges in faith, integrity, and responsibility. Founded on the Five-Fold Ministry—Apostles, Prophets, Evangelists, Pastors, and Teachers—FLY-M seeks to equip young people to handle life issues with wisdom and purpose. Committed to non-political, Christ-centered guidance, FLY-M believes in the authority of the Bible, salvation through Jesus, and the transformative power of the Holy Spirit in believers' lives.

Founders

Guiding a Generation with Purpose

At Feed My Lambs Youth Ministry (FLY-M), our heart beats for a generation of young people equipped to walk uprightly in their spiritual, professional, and social lives. Rooted in Biblical principles, we strive to guide the youth toward a personal relationship with Jesus Christ, empowering them to navigate life’s challenges with faith, integrity, and responsibility. Through our outreach programs across universities, high schools, and primary schools, we bring a holistic approach to spiritual growth and social impact. Inspired by the Five-Fold Ministry, we focus on instilling moral values, fostering emotional healing, and imparting professional orientation. Together, we address the pressing issues of our time, including substance abuse, violence, and relational challenges, to build a resilient and empowered generation. I invite you to join hands with us as we continue this God-ordained mission. Let us walk together, uplifting our youth to fulfill their divine purpose and positively impact society for the glory of His Kingdom.


Blessings

Presbyter Kato C. Lafont & Pastor Joan I. Lafont
Founders, Feed My Lamb Youth Ministry
