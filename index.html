About Us

Who Are We?

Feed My Lambs Youth Ministry (FLY-M) is an interdenominational fellowship dedicated to guiding young people in their spiritual, professional, and social journeys. Rooted in Biblical principles, FLY-M aims to help youth build a personal relationship with Jesus while empowering them to navigate life’s challenges in faith, integrity, and responsibility.

Our Impact

"As a member of Breach Repairers, I found a spiritual home that empowers me to tackle both academic and life challenges with faith and wisdom. The fellowship fosters a deep connection with Christ while encouraging professional and personal growth. Through seminars, mentorship, and outreach activities, I've learned to approach life with purpose, embracing integrity and responsibility. FLY-M has been instrumental in shaping my character and preparing me to step confidently into the future, knowing that I’m equipped with the values and tools to succeed."

Grace Mutu
Breach Repairers Club Member

"Joining Junior Radicals has been life-changing for me. FLY-M has provided a safe space where I can grow spiritually and emotionally, surrounded by supportive peers and mentors. The teachings and outreach activities help me understand the importance of living with integrity and staying true to my faith in a world filled with challenges like peer pressure and substance abuse. I’ve found strength in Jesus and have gained confidence in sharing my faith with others. Junior Radicals is more than just a group—it's a family that has helped me build a solid foundation for life."

Stephen Mwangi
Junior Radical Mentee
